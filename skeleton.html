THIS IS HOW IT WORKS

ORDER GROCERIES

Fram is a circular service that ensures your kitchen is always stocked with the essentials you need. Browse our seasonal selection, choose your items, and place your order online.

See our stock here
STOCK UP

Once your order arrives, unpack the produce and store it however it works best for you. Hold on to the containers- they're yours to keep until your next order.

SCAN & REORDER

Ready for a refill? Grab the containers from your last order and scan the QR code with our app on your phone's camera. This instantly adds the items back to your cart, making reordering a breeze.

SWAP & REUSE

When we deliver your new order, simply leave your empty containers outside. We'll pick them up, clean them thuroughly, and reuse them for future deliveries.
